# Retail Revenue Recovery Case Study

## Summary Report

This case study presents a realistic, end-to-end analytics engagement for a mid-sized retail company experiencing a decline in revenue and rising customer churn after a price increase and a weaker promotional calendar.

### Business Goal
Reduce churn, recover lost revenue, and identify the customer segments that should receive targeted retention offers.

### What This Case Study Includes
- A clear business problem statement
- A structured solution approach
- SQL scripts for analysis and reporting
- Visualization recommendations for stakeholder presentation
- A concise summary report suitable for portfolio or interview use

### Suggested Outcome
The analysis should help leadership answer four questions:
1. Which customer segments are most at risk of churn?
2. Which products or channels are driving the revenue decline?
3. Which interventions are likely to recover revenue fastest?
4. How should performance be tracked after the recovery plan is launched?
