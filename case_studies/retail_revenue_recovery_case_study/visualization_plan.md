# Visualization Plan

## Executive Dashboard
1. Revenue trend line chart by month
2. Active customer count by month
3. Churn risk distribution by customer segment
4. Revenue contribution by product category
5. Channel performance comparison bar chart

## Recommended Chart Types
- Line chart for revenue and customer growth trends
- Stacked bar chart for segment-level revenue contribution
- Funnel or heatmap for churn risk by segment and channel
- Scatter plot for average order value versus repeat purchase rate

## Story Flow for Presentation
1. Show the revenue drop over time.
2. Highlight the segment most affected by churn.
3. Reveal the product and channel drivers.
4. Recommend targeted interventions with expected impact.

## Design Notes
- Use a neutral executive palette with green for recovery and red for risk.
- Keep the view simple with a single page summary and drill-down links.
- Display confidence intervals or estimated uplift where possible.
